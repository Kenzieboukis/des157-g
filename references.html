The elements in this site, though possibly useful, are not asthetic. I believe there is a certain amount of interactivity that could help users understand more about the conflict at hand.

Jewish Voice for Peace

The Jewish Voice for Peace is a more biased telling of the Israel and Palestinian conflict. The page promotes peace in the Middle East but more from a Jewish perpective instead of a truly unbiased one. The page has a good intro video and when ones scrolls there are a lot of Q and As on the home page. The site is actually very well made expect the intro doesn't look as good as the rest of the scrolling does. There is also no interactivity in this page that I would like to put into mine. I really like the Q and A style format that the creators used on their homepage. I think it would be a good idea if I have an entire Q and A page on my site. I also like the simple and elegant colors the creators used in their creation of their site. I think it is a good way to lend important information to their readers.
